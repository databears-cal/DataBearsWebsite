General Members

DataBears is here to serve the data science community who is interested in further developing their data science skills with peers through hands on experience with projects that are found in DATA 100 and beyond. We will have fun exploring different dataset each week during Spring 2019 semester!

Interest Form

Data Campus Consulting

Our team is looking to provide consulting services to campus groups. We aim to provide our services to users interested in optimizing their businesses/services by generating, understanding and analyzing their data on a deeper level. We’re looking for people to further develop infrastructure for our platform; to implement, collect and analyze data to deliver results for our customers.

Data Education

Our team is dedicated to inspire data science and skills in high school students. We aim to reach as many students as possible through direct and indirect teaching initiatives. We are looking for motivated students with backgrounds in data science, especially if you have teaching experiences as an AI or UGSI.

Data Science Platform

We provide infrastructure and services that campus groups can use to generate, collect and understand the data. Tentatively we’re currently working on launching our Jupyterhub server and Consulting service.

For the infrastructure team, we look for:

  • System Administration/DevOps/SRE Background Knowledge (not required but a plus)
  • A "Get Stuff Done" Mindset
  • Good Communication Skills
  • Ability to Triage Problems by Isolating Variables

Consulting Services

Our team will provide services to campus groups to help student groups maximize their usage of data. Most importantly though, we look for someone who is passionate about helping other student organization succeed through insights from data.

For the Consulting Team, we look for:

  • Analytical Skills
  • Being Able to Work Profesionally with Other Campus Organizations
  • Ability to Find Exploratory Solutions to Given Problem
  • Data 100 (a plus)
  • Good Communication Skills
