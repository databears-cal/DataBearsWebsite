DataBears is here to serve the data science community who is interested in further developing their data science with peers through hands on experience. We go through casual projects in exploring interesting data sets to do general analysis.

Our team is looking to provide consulting services to campus groups. We aim to provide our services to users interested in optimizing their businesses or understanding their data on a deeper level. We’re looking for people to further develop infrastructure for our platform, as well as people to collect and analyze data to deliver results for our customers.

Our team is dedicated to improving the data literacy and skills of high school students. We aim to reach as many students as possible through direct and indirect teaching initiatives.
